About

Devun Shunta Brown

Entrepreneur · Air Force Veteran · Former USPS

Devun Shunta Brown is a dynamic entrepreneur whose journey from the U.S. Air Force to small business ownership is marked by resilience and reinvention. After serving in the military, Devun transitioned to the U.S. Postal Service, where she developed a deep appreciation for service and community. Her pivot into Pure Romance was driven by a desire to help women feel empowered, educated, and confident.

Devun brings military precision to her business, running it with a strategic mindset and a people-first heart. She’s a natural leader who thrives on making others feel seen and celebrated. Whether she’s hosting a product party, mentoring new consultants, or organizing women’s wellness events, Devun shows up with intention and authenticity.

Outside of work, Devun enjoys line dancing, reading romance novels, and spending time with her family. She was the point guard on her high school and college basketball teams — a fact that surprises many. Looking forward, she’s focused on growing a regional team of reps and hosting women’s empowerment retreats.
